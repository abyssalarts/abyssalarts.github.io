Philosophy

Privacy is the foundation, not a feature.

Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly choose otherwise. No analytics. No tracking. No cloud sync you didn't ask for.

One-time purchases over subscriptions.

If the app works offline and doesn't cost us server time, why would we charge you monthly? You buy it. It's yours.

No ads. Ever.

Not at launch. Not "for now." Not with an opt-out. Never.

Neurodivergent by default.

These tools aren't "accessible" as an afterthought — they're built by a neurodivergent developer for neurodivergent users. The UX accounts for executive dysfunction, sensory sensitivity, and brains that don't work the way textbooks say they should.

No dark patterns.

No fake urgency. No guilt-trip modals. No engagement metrics disguised as features. No "are you SURE you want to leave?"

About

Abyssal Arts is a solo Android development studio based in Florida. One developer. No office. No investors. No pressure to grow for growth's sake.

The name comes from the deep end — the place where the mind goes when it's quiet and the thoughts are too honest to say out loud. Every app in this catalog is built there, at hours most people are asleep, by someone who needed these tools to exist and got tired of waiting for someone else to build them.

The app ecosystem is broken. Most apps exist to extract — attention, data, money, all three. They wrap surveillance in convenience and call it a feature. Abyssal Arts exists because someone should be building the other thing.
