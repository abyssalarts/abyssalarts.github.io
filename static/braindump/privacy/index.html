BRAIN DUMP

PRIVACY POLICY  —  LAST UPDATED: MAY 2026
OVERVIEW

Brain Dump was built on a simple principle: your thoughts belong to you. Your thoughts are stored only on your device and are never sent anywhere except the AI provider you choose. The one thing we collect — only if you allow it — is anonymous usage counts (which screens and buttons get used, never your thoughts) to help us improve the app.

WHAT WE COLLECT

Almost nothing — and never your thoughts.

Your thoughts, voice, transcriptions, and API keys are never collected or transmitted by us. The only thing we collect is anonymous usage analytics, and only if you allow it (see Analytics, below).

  • ✕ No personal information (no name, email, account, or device ID)
  • ✕ Never your thoughts, voice, or transcriptions
  • ✕ No crash reporting
  • ✕ No advertising identifiers
  • ✕ No location data
  • ✕ No behavioral tracking or profiling
  • ✓ Anonymous usage counts — only with your permission (see Analytics)
ANALYTICS

Anonymous, optional, and never your thoughts.

To understand how Brain Dump is used and improve it, the app can send anonymous usage events — for example, that a screen was opened or a button was tapped. These contain no thought content, no personal identifiers, and no device ID. They are processed by Aptabase, a privacy-focused analytics provider, on EU-based infrastructure.

You control this. In the EU, EEA, and UK it is off until you opt in; elsewhere it is on by default after the onboarding disclosure and can be turned off at any time in Settings → Privacy → Share anonymous usage stats.

  • ✓ Anonymous counts only (events like “app opened”) — never thought content
  • ✓ No user, account, or device identifier — sessions are random and rotating
  • ✓ EU/EEA/UK: opt-in. Elsewhere: opt-out, toggleable in Settings anytime
  • ✓ Sent over an encrypted connection to Aptabase (EU region)
  • ✓ The app also keeps simple usage counts on your device that never leave it
WHERE YOUR DATA LIVES

Only on your device.

All thoughts, categories, connections, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. Nothing is sent to any server. Nothing is stored in the cloud unless you explicitly choose to export it yourself.

  • ✓ Thoughts stored locally on device only
  • ✓ Voice transcription processed locally or via your chosen provider
  • ✓ No automatic cloud sync or backup
  • ✓ Export is always your choice, always your destination
AI PROVIDERS

Optional. Your choice. Your key.

Brain Dump optionally supports AI categorization and analysis via third-party providers including Claude (Anthropic), OpenAI, Google Gemini, and Ollama. This feature is entirely opt-in and requires you to provide your own API key.

When AI features are enabled, your thought content is sent to your chosen provider for processing. This is governed by that provider's own privacy policy — not ours. We do not store, log, or access your API key beyond what is needed to make the request.

Ollama users: all processing happens locally on your device. No data leaves your machine.

  • ✓ AI features are fully optional
  • ✓ You provide your own API key
  • ✓ We never store or share your API key
  • ✓ Ollama option is fully local — zero external calls
  • ✕ We do not have access to thoughts processed by AI providers
THIRD PARTIES

Only what you choose.

The only third parties that ever receive data are the AI provider you pick (Claude, OpenAI, or Gemini — only when you trigger AI processing; Ollama stays fully local), and Aptabase for anonymous usage analytics, only while you have that enabled. No third party ever receives your thoughts for analytics.

  • ✕ No advertising networks
  • ✕ No crash reporting services
  • ✕ No social media integrations
  • ✕ No data brokers or data-sharing agreements
  • ✓ Aptabase (anonymous analytics, EU) — only while you opt in / stay opted in
YOUR CONTROL

Complete.

You have full control over your data at all times.

  • ✓ Export all thoughts at any time (JSON, Markdown, Obsidian, ZIP)
  • ✓ Delete individual thoughts at any time
  • ✓ Wipe all data completely from Settings → Privacy & Data → Clear All Data
  • ✓ Turn anonymous analytics off at any time in Settings → Privacy
  • ✓ Uninstalling the app removes all local data permanently
CHILDREN

Not directed at children.

Brain Dump is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Since we collect no information from anyone, this is straightforward.

CHANGES

We'll tell you.

If this privacy policy ever changes, we will update the date above and note the changes in the app's release notes. We will never retroactively change our data practices in ways that are less private than what is described here.
